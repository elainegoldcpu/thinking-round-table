Who this is for

This session is for you if
  • You're already using AI — or your organisation expects you to
  • You care about quality, tone, and reputation
  • You want clearer boundaries: what to delegate, what to review, what stays human-led
  • You want a calmer, more deliberate way to make decisions involving AI
This session is not for you if
  • You want beginner AI training
  • You want a prompt-writing class
  • You want tool walkthroughs, features, or technical troubleshooting

What to expect

A structured hour of guided reflection and open discussion. You will not be asked to share confidential information. Bring a real challenge if you have one — or simply listen and reflect.

0–10 mins Framing — AI as drafting partner; humans remain accountable
10–25 mins Guided reflection — your workflow, tasks, and review points
25–45 mins Round table discussion — shared patterns, risks, and what "good" looks like
45–55 mins Synthesis — principles you can apply immediately
55–60 mins Next steps — optional ways to go deeper

The themes we'll explore

  • Hybrid working — how decisions change when AI drafts
  • Standards — what "good" looks like when AI is involved
  • Review and sign-off — how to reduce over-checking without increasing risk
  • Delegation — right tasks, right tools, right instructions
  • Confidence — why capable people still hesitate, and what helps

What you'll leave with

  • Clearer judgement about where AI fits in your work
  • Practical boundaries you can apply immediately
  • A simple review discipline that protects quality and reputation
  • Language you can use with colleagues and teams
